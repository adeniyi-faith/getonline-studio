"Anchor text" just means the words that are clickable in a link. If someone writes "check out this web design company in Abuja" — those underlined words are the anchor. Varying these is important so Google doesn't think you're gaming the system.

How to split your anchor text

45%

City + Service phrases

The most important for your pSEO. Things like "web design Lagos", "website designer Enugu", "digital agency Kano". These directly push your city pages up in search.

25%

Your brand name

Just "GetOnline Studio" or "getonlinestudio.com". This builds brand recognition and looks natural to Google.

30%

General / natural phrases

Things like "affordable web design Nigeria", "best website company", "click here", "learn more". These make the whole link profile look human and normal.

Simple rule: If you're getting 10 backlinks this month — 4 or 5 should say a city name, 2 or 3 should say "GetOnline Studio", and the rest should be natural-sounding phrases. Don't use the exact same words every time.
