Client Musician & Artist
Role Design & Dev
Style Vibrant Minimalist

( THE VIBE )

The Vision: Create a digital space that feels like the artist's music: vibrant, futuristic, and clean. It serves as a central hub for fans to listen to tracks, view album art, and connect.

The Atmosphere: Instead of heavy textures, we used deep color gradients to evoke a night sky or club atmosphere, punctuated by pulsing motions that make the site feel "alive."
