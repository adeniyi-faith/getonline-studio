The Story

A lending business running on notebooks and phone calls.

OA Global Standard Services gives out loans to everyday Nigerians — salary workers, small business owners, and people who need financial support between paychecks. Good business idea. Real demand. Loyal clients.

But behind the scenes, things were messy. Client information lived in notebooks. Repayment tracking happened on WhatsApp. Nobody could tell at a glance who owed what, when it was due, or which loans were overdue. When a new client came in, staff had to dig through old files just to get started.

They came to us with one request: "Help us get organised."

We built them a complete web-based loan management system — a central place where staff can register clients, issue loans, track repayments, and see everything in one screen. Simple to use. Built for how their business actually works.

Before We Came In

What was going wrong

These are the real problems OA Global faced before the system existed.

Paper client files

New clients filled out physical forms. Those forms got lost, damaged, or were just impossible to find quickly when you needed them.

No loan tracking

There was no single place to see all active loans. Staff had to call clients or dig through notes to find out what was owed and when.

Missed repayments

Without a system to flag overdue loans, some clients fell behind without the business realising until much later.

Staff had no clear roles

Different staff members could see — or change — things they shouldn't. There were no proper access levels to keep information secure.

No real overview

Management had no quick way to answer basic questions — how many loans are active? What is the total amount outstanding?

Clients had no access

Borrowers had to call or visit the office to check their loan balance, repayment history, or due dates. Inconvenient for everyone.

How It Works

The loan journey, step by step

Here is exactly how a loan goes from application to full repayment inside the system. Every step is done by a real person on the staff team — the system just keeps it all organised and recorded.

01

Client walks in

A new borrower comes to the office. Staff open the admin panel and click "New Client." They fill in all the client's details — personal info, employment, monthly income, guarantor info, and collateral — in one digital form. The system saves everything instantly and creates a secure account for the client.

Personal Details Employment Info Guarantor Collateral Bank Details
02

Loan is approved and issued

When management approves the loan, staff open the disbursement screen, search for the client by name, pick the loan product (salary advance, SME loan, etc.), enter the amount and repayment period, and confirm. The system calculates the repayment schedule, management fees, and due date — and logs everything automatically.

Loan Products Fee Calculation Due Date Repayment Plan
03

Client checks their portal

Once the loan is issued, the client gets login details for their personal portal. They can log in from any phone or computer to see their current balance, their repayment history, how much they still owe, and when their next payment is due. No need to call the office.

Loan Balance Payment History Due Dates Savings Balance
04

Client makes a payment

When a client pays — either at the office or by bank transfer — staff go to the repayments screen, search for the client, enter the amount paid, and log it. The system updates the client's loan balance right away and records the date and who logged it. If a client uploads a payment receipt in their portal, staff get an alert to review and approve it.

Manual Log Receipt Upload Balance Update Activity Log
✓

Loan is fully repaid

Once the balance hits zero, the system automatically marks the loan as fully paid. The client's record stays in the system — so if they come back for another loan, staff can see their full history in seconds. Overdue loans are flagged automatically so management always knows who needs a follow-up.

Auto-Marked Paid Overdue Flags Full History
Features

Everything inside the system

Six major pieces — all working together.

Client Registration

Staff register new clients using a structured multi-step form. Personal details, employment, income, guarantor, collateral documents, and bank account — all saved in one client profile that never gets lost.

Loan Disbursement

Staff search for a client, pick a loan product, enter the amount, and issue the loan. The system logs the disbursement date, repayment period, management fee, and expected repayment — all at once.

Repayment Tracking

Every payment gets logged — who paid, how much, and when. The system updates the outstanding balance immediately. Overdue loans are flagged automatically so nothing slips through.

Client Portal

Each client gets their own login to a clean dashboard. They can see their current loan, outstanding balance, repayment history, savings balance, and any available loan products — from any device.

Activity Log

Every action in the system is recorded — who did what, and when. Management can always go back and see a full history of changes, payments logged, and client updates. Full accountability.

Staff Roles & Access

Not everyone can do everything. Admins control the whole system. Other staff members only see what they need — like client management or repayment logging. The right people access the right screens.

Feature Spotlight

A loan calculator that builds trust before the client even applies

One of the biggest barriers in lending is fear of hidden fees. People want to know exactly what they will pay back before they commit.

We built a live loan calculator into the public website. A visitor types in how much they want to borrow and how long they need to pay it back — and the calculator instantly shows them the management fee, total repayment, and monthly breakdown.

Complete transparency. No surprises. More confident applicants.
